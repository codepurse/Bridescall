Micro Wedding

We call them micro wedding packages, others call them a small wedding or an elopement. No matter what word you use, to us it's an intimate wedding with some, or all, of the traditional wedding elements. Simple, casual or elegant, celebrations that make you feel like it's your wedding, and not a trip to the courthouse.

But, as you may have already found out, planning a smaller wedding, especially in a tiny beach town like Saugatuck, isn't as easy as we make it look or sound. So we make it easy for you, our wedding packages are filled all the wedding basics that most couples ask for when they get married.
